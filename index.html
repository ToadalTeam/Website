Toadal Performance is a team-based fitness coaching service built on Long Island. Instead of working with one coach, you get three — NASM, ISSA, and ACE certified — who collaborate on every aspect of your program.

Whether you are on Long Island, in New York City, or anywhere in the world, our remote coaching, custom programs, and nutrition guidance are available to you. Distance is not a barrier.

EAMON MURRAY
Co-Founder · Coach · Long Island, NY
Eamon is a NASM Certified Personal Trainer from Long Island with a unique fitness journey. As a child he struggled with severe arthritis that significantly limited his physical abilities. Through consistent training and determination, he overcame those challenges and went on to become a multi-sport All-State and All-American athlete.
