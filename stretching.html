Pre-WorkoutUse dynamic stretches before training. Never hold static stretches cold — move through the range instead.
Post-WorkoutHold static stretches for 45-90 seconds when muscles are warm. This is when flexibility gains actually happen.
Daily MobilityThese can be done any time of day. Even 10 minutes daily compounds significantly over weeks and months.
